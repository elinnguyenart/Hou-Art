About the Artist

Guanghui Hou is a classical realist painter specializing in the traditions of 19th-century European academic art. Originating from China and rigorously trained at the Angel Academy of Art in Florence, his artistic vision is deeply enriched by his background in European history, art, and theater. This multidisciplinary foundation brings a unique narrative depth and a profound reverence for the Old Masters to his canvases.

His growing portfolio encompasses sensitive portraits, elegant figurative works, and carefully observed still lifes, all united by a dedication to accurate draftsmanship and refined realism. As an emerging artist establishing his studio practice, Hou is passionately committed to carrying the discipline of the historic ateliers forward into a new generation.
