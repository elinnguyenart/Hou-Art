Classes & Workshops

Instruction in Classical Realism

I offer private instruction and focused group workshops dedicated to the rigorous techniques of 19th-century European academic art. Rooted in the traditions of the historic Florentine ateliers, my curriculum is designed to guide dedicated artists through the foundational disciplines of classical drawing and traditional oil painting.

The curriculum follows a strict, time-honored progression. Students begin by mastering accurate optical observation through Charles Bargue plate drawings in pencil. Training then advances to charcoal to build a rigorous understanding of form and value, before finally culminating in the traditional indirect oil painting methods of the Old Masters.
